## What UI and UX Actually Mean  

UI (User Interface) focuses on the visual elements of a digital product such as:

- Layout structure  
- Buttons and navigation  
- Typography and colors  
- Interactive components  

UX (User Experience) focuses on how users interact with the system, including:

- Ease of navigation  
- Logical flow of information  
- Page speed and responsiveness  
- Overall satisfaction during interaction  

Together, UI and UX determine whether users stay on your website or leave within seconds.
