## Website Cost in Nepal: 2026 Pricing Overview  

Website cost depends on type, complexity, and the agency you choose. Here’s a realistic breakdown:

### 1. Basic Business Website  
- 5–7 pages  
- Mobile responsive  
- Contact form integration  
- Basic SEO setup  
**Cost:** NPR 20,000 – 50,000  

### 2. Corporate or Professional Website  
- 10–20 pages  
- Custom UI/UX design  
- CMS integration  
- Speed and SEO optimization  
**Cost:** NPR 50,000 – 200,000  

### 3. eCommerce Website  
- Product management system  
- Payment gateway integration (Khalti, eSewa)  
- Order tracking & inventory system  
**Cost:** NPR 200,000 – 300,000+  

### 4. Custom Web Application  
- Dashboards, portals, or SaaS applications  
- Advanced backend integration  
- Scalable architecture for future growth  
**Cost:** NPR 300,000 – 1,000,000+  

> Note: Extremely low-cost websites often rely on templates, skip SEO, or lack long-term support.

## Factors Affecting Website Cost in Nepal  

Several factors influence the final pricing:

1. **Design Complexity** – Custom designs cost more than templates.  
2. **Number of Pages** – More pages require additional design and development time.  
3. **Functionality** – Features like eCommerce, booking systems, or dashboards increase cost.  
4. **SEO & Performance Optimization** – Websites optimized for search engines require extra work.  
5. **Maintenance & Support** – Post-launch updates, security, and hosting plans.  

A professional agency clearly communicates all these factors and provides transparent quotes.
